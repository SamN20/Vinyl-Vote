ℹ️ About Song Ignore Feature

  • When a song is marked as ignored, all votes for that song are automatically ignored
  • Users can still cast votes on ignored songs, but they won't count toward averages or results
  • Toggle the ignore status to instantly update all associated votes
  • Songs marked with a SHORT badge are under 1:30 in duration (often interludes or skits)
  • Useful for excluding interludes, skits, or problematic tracks from calculations
  • Ignored songs are clearly marked and their stats remain visible for reference
